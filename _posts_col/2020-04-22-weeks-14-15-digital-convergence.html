Readings and Assignments

  • Read "Taroko Gorge" by Nick Montfort
    • Write a 1 paragraph analysis of this poem. What is Montfort trying to say with the poem and why did he choose to end it as he did?
  • Explore Collection: Bots
    • Write a 1 paragraph discussion of the Twitter bots you found most interesting in this collection.
  • Read "How to Rob a Bank" by Alan Bigelow
    • Write a 1 paragraph discussion of how Bigelow incorporates multimedia in meaningful ways in this work.
  • Read "The Hunt for the Gay Planet" by Anna Anthropy
    • Write a 1 paragraph discussion of the use of interactivity and game elements in this work.
  • Watch my public lectures "Turning the Page" and "Distant Writing," available here.
    • Write a 1 paragraph response to each lecture, discussing how it relates to this unit and the course.
