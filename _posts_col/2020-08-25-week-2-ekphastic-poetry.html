    • Instructions:
      • Identify which group you've been placed in.
      • Read and use hypothes.is to annotate the poem you've been assigned to.
      • Write a 1-page (about 250 words) analysis of the poem in conversation with the painting it alludes to. Focus on how the poet is interpreting the painting and what they wish to express in response to it. This is due by Thursday at 5 pm.
      • Look at your group mates' posts and respond to their posts, seeking to have a conversation about how you're all interpreting the works.
      • Go to the other 3 groups and respond, substantively, to an analysis by one your classmates in each group. This is due before Tuesday.
